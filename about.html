About Us

Desire is a furniture-specialized company that has been operating more than 10 years in furniture industry

Desire furniture company has been operating since 2010, Located in Uzbekistan, Tashkent. Desire has more than 10 years experience of manufacturing and exporting in hotel furniture, banquet furniture, villa furniture, apartment and outdoor furniture. Our product include: Sofa, Coffee & Side Tables, Consoles, Chests, Dining Tables, Dining Chairs, Beds, Desks and so on.

We are able to design to new products customer's specification. If you need special product, please give us your drawing or your idea. We will design for you. Competitive prices and excellent service with a good reputation in the market. I trust you will be happy to cooperate with us.
